About us

Sindico are one of the largest suppliers of equipment for wastewater treatment in New Zealand and the leading supplier of Huber Technology from Germany.

Since 1984 Sindico has been providing engineered solutions in Wastewater treatment with a comprehensive range of technologies and equipment.

Some of our services include:

Dewatering

Sindico are the NZ agents for Huber Technology and Pieralisi. We have a dewatering machine to suit any application.

Screening

Sindico have installed more than 120 screening units in New Zealand and Australia with a proven track record of excellence.

Clarification

Sindico manufacture and are agents for a wide range of clarifiers. We have the right clarifier for your application.

Grit Treatment

From classifiers to grit removers, Sindico have the right grit separation and washing systems for your project.

Filtration

Sindico are the NZ agents for Nordic Water and Huber Technology who each manufacturer filtration systems suitable for most applications.

Aerators

Sindico are the sole NZ agent for Mississippi USA Aerator Manufacturers S&N Airoflo, provders of efficient, predictable wastewater treatment.

Brands we supply

Sindico are suppliers of the leading brands in water treatment

HUBER SE

The Quality wastewater Company Worldwide.

HUBER SE, headquartered in Berching, Germany, is a worldwide active company in the field of water, wastewater and sludge treatment. HUBER equipment is 100% German made.

At our headquarters in Berching, more than 600 employees develop and manufacture products, manage projects and develop system solutions for municipalities and industries.

With more than 29,000 installations worldwide HUBER is one of the internationally leading companies in this field.

HUBER's adapted treatment processes contribute to the solution of global water problems.

HUBER supports its customers in about 60 countries around the world through subsidiaries, offices or representatives by providing know-how and innovative products for water, wastewater and sludge treatment.

Water, wastewater and energy

We offer our customers systems and services together with products that can purify, save and protect water. We have processes to utilize waste energy and sludge.

Environmental Responsibility

Our products and services contribute to protecting the environment and are in accordance with the principles of sustainability. It is our aim to meet our social responsibilities.
